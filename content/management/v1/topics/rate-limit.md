---
title: Rate Limit
---

The management api is rate limited to 3 requests per second for users on the free plan and 6 requests per second for other users.

;examplearea

| Plan | Rate Limit |
|------|-----------|
| Community | 3 per second |
| Entry | 6 per second |
| Business | 6 per second |
| Enterprise | Custom |
| Enterprise Plus | Custom |
